# Privacy Policy for Battery Drainer

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Overview

Battery Drainer ("the App") is a battery benchmarking tool developed by Stefan Rattay. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

## Data Collection

**Battery Drainer does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Don't Do
- ❌ We do NOT collect personal information
- ❌ We do NOT track your location history
- ❌ We do NOT send data to external servers (except for optional test downloads)
- ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- ❌ We do NOT share any data with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT create user accounts

### What Stays On Your Device
- ✅ All battery test data
- ✅ Generated reports
- ✅ Settings and preferences
- ✅ Test history

## Permissions Explained

The App requests certain permissions for stress testing functionality only:

| Permission | Why We Need It | Data Storage |
|------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Location (GPS)** | GPS stress testing to simulate navigation apps | Not stored, immediately discarded |
| **Camera** | Flashlight/torch control for stress testing | No images captured |
| **Internet** | Network stress testing by downloading test files | No data sent from device |
| **Vibration** | Vibration motor stress testing | N/A |
| **Foreground Service** | Run tests in background without being killed | N/A |
| **Wake Lock** | Keep device awake during tests | N/A |

## Network Usage

When using the Network Stressor feature:
- The App downloads test files from public speed test servers
- No personal data is uploaded
- Downloads are immediately discarded after reading
- You can disable this feature at any time

## Data Storage

All data created by the App (test reports, settings) is stored locally on your device in:
- App-specific storage (automatically deleted when app is uninstalled)
- No cloud backup
- No remote storage

## Third-Party Services

Battery Drainer does NOT use any third-party services such as:
- Analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising networks
- Social media SDKs

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for developers and QA professionals.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
